# Country OSINT — Hungary (Magyarország)

Hungarian-language EU country with sharply-restricted media environment under the Orbán government (since 2010). Most independent investigative work happens at a handful of remaining outlets, often under legal pressure.

---

## Languages & Search

- **Hungarian (Magyar)** — Uralic language, unique in Europe; agglutinative, Latin script with extra letters (á, é, í, ó, ö, ő, ú, ü, ű)
- **English** common in business
- Translation: Google Translate / DeepL — Hungarian is difficult, results often need verification
- **Google.hu** — dominant

## News & Media

> Hungary's media is heavily controlled — most national outlets are owned by KESMA (Central European Press and Media Foundation), a Fidesz-aligned foundation that absorbed ~500 outlets in 2018.

### Pro-government / KESMA
- **Magyar Nemzet** — once-independent, now Fidesz-aligned
- **Origo** — government-aligned
- **HírTV** — government-aligned TV

### Independent (mostly online)
- **Telex** ([telex.hu](https://telex.hu/)) — formed 2020 after Index.hu was taken over; major independent
- **HVG** ([hvg.hu](https://hvg.hu/)) — weekly + online, centre-left
- **444.hu** ([444.hu](https://444.hu/)) — online, irreverent
- **24.hu** — online news
- **Magyar Hang** — opposition weekly

### Investigative
- **Direkt36** ([direkt36.hu](https://www.direkt36.hu/)) — flagship investigative non-profit, OCCRP member — exposed Pegasus deployment, Orbán family business, etc.
- **Atlatszo** ([atlatszo.hu](https://atlatszo.hu/)) — investigative + FOI specialists
- **Forbes Hungary investigations**

### English-language
- **Daily News Hungary, Hungary Today**
- **Telex.hu English**

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Pegasus** — confirmed Hungarian-government use against journalists, lawyers, opposition (2021 Pegasus Project disclosure)
- **Orbán family business** — Direkt36 sustained investigations
- **Russian intelligence presence (IIB - International Investment Bank)** — Direkt36 + investigators tracked Russian-aligned diplomatic entities in Budapest before its 2023 relocation
- **EU funds misuse** — Atlatszo + EUobserver tracking
- **Hungarian-US far-right cross-pollination** — CPAC Hungary, Tucker Carlson visits, ideological exports
- **Sanctions exemption advocacy on Russian energy** — sustained OSINT scrutiny

---

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Press freedom severely restricted** — RSF ranks Hungary among the worst in EU
- **Sovereignty Protection Office (2023)** — broad surveillance / interference-investigation powers
- **Defamation** is criminal
- **Pegasus victims include investigators** — threat-modeling matters for sensitive subjects
- **EU funds suspension (2023+)** is a recurring backdrop
