# Country OSINT — Romania (România)

Romanian-language EU country with disproportionately strong cybercrime / OCG OSINT context, Russia-adjacent black-sea positioning, and a notable investigative-journalism scene.

---

## Languages & Search

- **Romanian (Română)** — Latin script with diacritics (ă, â, î, ș, ț)
- **Hungarian** — significant minority in Transylvania
- **English** widely spoken
- **Google.ro** — dominant

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Romanian-Moldovan organised crime** — well-documented
- **Cybercrime (Hackerville / Râmnicu Vâlcea)** — Romanian fraud / carding ecosystems
- **RISE Project investigations** — Putin's family, Russian / Iranian sanctions evasion via Romanian routes
- **Andrew Tate case** — Romanian prosecution, OSINT-heavy
- **Russia-Ukraine border / Ukrainian refugee monitoring** — Romania is a major refugee-host country
- **Black Sea grain corridor** — shipping OSINT
