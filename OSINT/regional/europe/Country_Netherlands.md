# Country OSINT — Netherlands (Nederland)

The Netherlands has an exceptionally **transparent corporate registry** (KVK), strong investigative-journalism scene, and a disproportionately large OSINT analyst community — Bellingcat, Lighthouse Reports, and several major investigative orgs are Amsterdam-based.

---

## Languages & Search

- **Dutch (Nederlands)** — Latin script, no diacritics
- **Frisian (Frysk)** — co-official in Friesland; minor OSINT relevance
- Most Dutch professionals work fluently in English; English-language Dutch news is widespread
- **Google.nl** — dominant
- Translation: **DeepL** (excellent), Google Translate

## Government & Corporate Records

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **KVK (Kamer van Koophandel)** ([kvk.nl](https://www.kvk.nl/)) | Chamber of Commerce — companies, directors, officers. **Search free**, full documents via fee. Among the most transparent EU registries |
| **UBO Register** | Beneficial-ownership registry — was public until 2022 ECJ ruling; now restricted to "legitimate interest" |
| **AFM** ([afm.nl](https://www.afm.nl/)) | Financial regulator — listed-company filings |
| **DNB** | Central bank, FinTec |
| **Court records (Rechtspraak.nl)** ([rechtspraak.nl](https://www.rechtspraak.nl/)) | Court rulings — anonymised, searchable, free |
| **Staatscourant** ([staatscourant.officielebekendmakingen.nl](https://staatscourant.officielebekendmakingen.nl/)) | State Gazette — official notices |
| **Kadaster** | Land registry — paid per query, very rich |
| **PDOK** ([pdok.nl](https://www.pdok.nl/)) | Geo / cadastral open data |
| **Open data portal** ([data.overheid.nl](https://data.overheid.nl/)) | National open-data |
| **WOO requests** | Wet Open Overheid — Dutch FOIA |
| **Belastingdienst** (tax authority) | Limited public; corporate ID lookup |

> KVK searches are free for basic data; document downloads cost ~€2-5 each. Even the paid tier is cheap by international standards.

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **GDPR + Uitvoeringswet AVG** apply
- **Press freedom** is strong; **bronbescherming** (source protection) constitutionally enshrined
- **Defamation (smaad / laster)** is criminal but rarely prosecuted
- **Wet computercriminaliteit III (2019)** gives police OSINT-related powers
- **Investigatory journalism** has cultural and legal protection
