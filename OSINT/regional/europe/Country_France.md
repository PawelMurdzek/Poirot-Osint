# Country OSINT — France

France has a notably-rich corporate-records ecosystem (free!) and bridges into **francophone Africa** (Senegal, Mali, Côte d'Ivoire, DRC, Cameroon, Madagascar, etc.) where French OSINT methodology often applies.

## Languages

- **French (Français)** — Latin script with diacritics (à, é, è, ê, ë, î, ï, ô, ö, ù, û, ü, ÿ, ç)
- French has multiple regional variants — Metropolitan, Belgian, Swiss Romand, Quebec, francophone African — vocabulary differs
- Translation: **DeepL** (excellent), Google Translate, **Reverso** for slang/colloquial

## Government & Corporate Records

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **Pappers** ([pappers.fr](https://www.pappers.fr/)) | **Best free corporate-search tool for France** — aggregates SIREN/SIRET, full-text searchable, free + paid API |
| **INSEE / SIRENE** ([insee.fr/sirene](https://avis-situation-sirene.insee.fr/)) | National Statistics Institute — corporate identifiers (SIREN/SIRET) |
| **Infogreffe** ([infogreffe.fr](https://www.infogreffe.fr/)) | Commercial court registry — paid for documents |
| **BODACC** ([bodacc.fr](https://www.bodacc.fr/)) | Official Gazette of Civil & Commercial Acts — corporate notices, bankruptcies |
| **Société.com** | Corporate aggregator |
| **AMF** ([amf-france.org](https://www.amf-france.org/)) | Financial regulator — listed-company filings |
| **data.gouv.fr** | Open-data portal — extensive |
| **Cadastre** ([cadastre.gouv.fr](https://www.cadastre.gouv.fr/)) | Cadastral data — free public access |
| **DVF (Demandes de Valeurs Foncières)** | All real-estate transactions since 2014, with prices — free, very rich |
| **Court records** | Limited public access; **Légifrance** ([legifrance.gouv.fr](https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/)) for laws & high-court rulings |
| **CNIL** | Data-protection authority decisions |
| **HATVP** ([hatvp.fr](https://www.hatvp.fr/)) | High Authority for Transparency in Public Life — politicians' interest declarations + asset declarations |
| **Lobby Register** | EU Transparency Register + national |

> France's **DVF dataset** is exceptional — every real-estate transaction since 2014 with full price + parcel ID, free download. Used heavily by journalists for political-figure property tracking.

## Francophone Africa Bridge

French OSINT methodology often extends into West & Central Africa. Most francophone-African countries have:
- **French-language dominance** in media + government
- **Limited public-records infrastructure** (variable)
- **Strong French diaspora ties** in Paris/Marseille/Lyon
- **OHADA harmonised business law** in 17 countries — corporate registries via OHADA-RCCM where implemented

Key francophone countries with their own contexts:
- **Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon** — relatively transparent, growing OSINT communities
- **DRC, CAR, Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger** — conflict / weak-state contexts; Bellingcat case studies frequent
- **Madagascar, Comoros** — Indian Ocean francophone

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **GDPR + Loi Informatique et Libertés** apply — CNIL enforces firmly
- **Right to be forgotten** — France pushed the original CJEU ruling; enforcement is aggressive
- **Press freedom (loi 1881)** is strong but defamation can be criminal; "atteinte à la vie privée" (privacy violation) is a specific tort
- **Article 11 Charte des droits fondamentaux** — strong privacy protection
- **Surveillance** — Loi Renseignement (2015) gives French intelligence broad OSINT authority
- **Pegasus** revelations (2021) — French president's phone was among targeted; threat-modeling matters for sensitive-target work

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Disclose / French military procurement leaks** — sustained investigative work on French arms exports (Yemen, Egypt)
- **Mediapart political-corruption** — Sarkozy / Cahuzac / Fillon investigations all OSINT-heavy
- **Africa OSINT bridge** — Bellingcat + RFI + Le Monde Afrique cover francophone Africa together
- **Yellow Vests (Gilets Jaunes, 2018-2019)** — Facebook group monitoring + protest geolocation
