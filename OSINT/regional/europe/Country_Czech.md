# Country OSINT — Czech Republic (Česko)

Czech-language EU country, decent press environment, Russia-investigations relevance, deep-tech / antivirus industry hub (Avast, ESET-Slovak, Kaspersky-CZ-disconnected post-2022).

---

## Languages & Search

- **Czech (Čeština)** — Latin script with diacritics (č, ď, ě, ň, ř, š, ť, ž, ů, etc.)
- **Slovak** is mutually intelligible — combined searches valuable
- **English** widely spoken
- **Google.cz** — dominant; **Seznam.cz** ([seznam.cz](https://www.seznam.cz/)) — domestic search, ~25% market share

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Russian sanctions evasion via Czech middlemen** — well-documented
- **Vrbětice ammunition depot explosion (2014)** — GRU Unit 29155 attribution work, multi-outlet OSINT
- **Babiš / Stork's Nest case** — Pandora Papers + sustained investigation
- **Russian agent expulsions** — periodic episodes
- **Avast / antivirus industry OSINT** — Czech firms historically connected to global cyber-OSINT

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Press freedom** generally good
- **Defamation** is civil
- **BIS (Counter-Intelligence)** — domestic intel; periodic public reports useful
