# Country OSINT — Switzerland (Schweiz / Suisse / Svizzera / Svizra)

Switzerland's role in **banking secrecy, sanctions evasion, commodity trading, and offshore finance** makes it an outsized OSINT target despite its small size. Multilingual: German, French, Italian, Romansh.

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Swiss-Russian sanctions evasion** — sustained focus post-2022; most countries less aggressive than EU on this
- **Commodity-trading OSINT** — Glencore, Vitol, Trafigura, Mercuria HQs in Geneva/Zug; Public Eye has a long catalogue
- **Bank-secrecy lift / FATCA** — Credit Suisse, UBS investigations
- **FIFA / sports-governance OSINT** — Zurich-HQ context
- **Anti-money-laundering investigations** — periodic massive cases (1MDB, Petrobras, etc.)
- **Paradise / Pandora / Suisse Secrets papers** — all featured Swiss corporate / banking data heavily

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Swiss DPA + nFADP (revised 2023)** — GDPR-aligned but with Swiss specificities
- **Press freedom** strong
- **Defamation** can be criminal
- **Banking secrecy** — Article 47 Banking Act criminalises unauthorised disclosure; affects what insiders can leak
- **State surveillance (NDB)** — limited transparency
- **Switzerland is a frequent venue for Russian / sanctioned-individual asset litigation**
