# Country OSINT — Spain (España)

Spain bridges Europe and Latin America — Spanish-language methodology applies broadly across 20+ Spanish-speaking countries. Catalonia, Basque Country, and Galicia have distinct linguistic + political contexts.

---

## Languages

- **Spanish (Español/Castellano)** — dominant, Latin script with ñ, accents
- **Catalan (Català)** — Catalonia, Valencia, Balearic Islands
- **Basque (Euskara)** — Basque Country
- **Galician (Galego)** — Galicia
- Always run searches in **co-official** languages where relevant — political content especially
- Translation: DeepL, Reverso, Google Translate (all solid for ES)

---

## Search

- **Google.es** dominant
- **Bing España** niche
- Most major search engines have ES-tuned results

---

## Social Networks

Similar to Western Europe baseline:
- **WhatsApp** universal
- **Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube** mainstream
- **X / Twitter** smaller per-capita than Anglo countries; politically influential
- **Telegram** moderate use; some COVID-conspiracy / Vox-aligned channels
- **Mastodon** active community (`mastodon.social/es` and many ES instances)
- **Forocoches** ([forocoches.com](https://www.forocoches.com/)) — Spain's most influential anonymous forum, automotive-origin but covers everything; comparable to 4chan or Wykop in influence
- **Verkami / Goteo** — crowdfunding, OSINT-relevant for cause tracking
- **Idealista, Fotocasa** — real-estate listings

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **Registro Mercantil Central / BORME** ([rmc.es](https://www.rmc.es/) / [boe.es/diario_borme](https://boe.es/diario_borme/)) | Mercantile Registry + Official Gazette of Commercial Registry — corporate filings, paid per-document |

## Latin America Bridge

Most Spanish-language Latin American OSINT methodology overlaps with Spain's:
- Corporate-registry conventions vary widely (Argentina, Mexico, Colombia each have their own)
- Spanish-language news ecosystems often interconnected (El País Latam, Univisión)
- Diaspora/emigration links between Spain and Latin America are dense — useful for cross-border investigations

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **GDPR + LOPDGDD (national implementation)** apply
- **Right to be forgotten** enforced by AEPD (data-protection agency)
- **Press freedom (Article 20 Constitution)** strong but defamation can be criminal
- **Catalonia / Basque political-context** — some OSINT touches sensitive separatism / counter-terrorism issues; framework legacy from ETA era
- **Pegasus** — Spanish PM's phone targeted; threat-modeling for sensitive subjects relevant
