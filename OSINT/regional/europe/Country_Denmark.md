# Country OSINT — Denmark (Danmark)

Danish-language Nordic country, very high public-records transparency, EU jurisdiction, strong investigative-journalism tradition.

## Government & Corporate Records

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **CVR (Central Business Registry)** ([cvr.dk](https://datacvr.virk.dk/)) | Companies registry — **completely free, full data including financial statements**; among the most transparent EU registries |
| **Danish Business Authority** | Parent of CVR |
| **Finanstilsynet** | Financial supervisory authority |
| **Nasdaq Copenhagen** | Stock exchange |
| **Statstidende** ([statstidende.dk](https://www.statstidende.dk/)) | Official Gazette |
| **Domstol.dk** | Court records — selectively public |
| **Geodatastyrelsen** | Land/cadastral data |
| **Skat** (Tax) | Limited public; corporate VAT lookup |
| **Open Data Denmark** ([opendata.dk](https://www.opendata.dk/)) | Open-data portal |
| **Folketinget** | Parliament records |

> **CVR is one of the most analyst-friendly corporate registries globally** — full financial-statement downloads, director histories, beneficial-ownership data, all free.
