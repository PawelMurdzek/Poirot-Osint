# Country OSINT — Ireland (Éire)

Ireland punches massively above its weight in OSINT relevance because it hosts the **European HQs of Meta, Google, X/Twitter, TikTok, Apple, Microsoft, Amazon** and much of the EU tech-data flow. Strong corporate transparency, English-language, EU jurisdiction.

---

## Languages & Search

- **English** — dominant
- **Irish (Gaeilge)** — co-official; minor online presence but state documents may be in both
- **Google.ie** — dominant

## Government & Corporate Records

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **CRO (Companies Registration Office)** ([cro.ie](https://www.cro.ie/)) | Companies registry — **search free**, document downloads cost ~€2.50 each |
| **CORE** | CRO online lookup |
| **RFS (Register of Friendly Societies)** | Co-ops, credit unions |
| **Beneficial Ownership** | RBO (Register of Beneficial Owners) — restricted post-2022 ECJ ruling |
| **Companies Act filings** | Annual returns, financial statements |
| **Iris Oifigiúil** ([irisoifigiuil.ie](https://www.irisoifigiuil.ie/)) | Official State Gazette |
| **Court Service** ([courts.ie](https://www.courts.ie/)) | Court records — limited public access; Decisis database paid |
| **CIPO** ([ipoi.gov.ie](https://www.ipoi.gov.ie/)) | Patent / trademark |
| **Central Bank of Ireland** | Banking + securities regulator |
| **Irish Stock Exchange (Euronext Dublin)** | Listed-company filings |
| **Property Registration Authority (PRA)** | Land registry — paid lookup |
| **Property Price Register** | All residential transactions since 2010 — **free, very rich** |
| **Revenue.ie** | Tax authority; corporate VAT lookup |
| **Open Data Ireland** ([data.gov.ie](https://data.gov.ie/)) | Open-data portal |

> **CRO is one of the more transparent EU corporate registries** for documents — every Irish company files annual accounts, and downloads are cheap.

## Why Ireland Matters for Tech / Privacy OSINT

Ireland is the **Lead Supervisory Authority (LSA)** under GDPR for most US tech companies operating in Europe — Meta, Google, X, TikTok, Apple, Microsoft. This means:
- **DPC (Data Protection Commission)** ([dataprotection.ie](https://www.dataprotection.ie/)) — issues GDPR fines on US Big Tech (largely)
- **Major rulings** (Schrems II, Meta-€1.2B fine 2023, etc.) flow through Dublin
- **Tax-structures OSINT** — Irish-incorporated holdcos are sanctioned-Russian-money endpoint research staple
- **Corporate-tax investigations** — "Double Irish" / "Single Malt" tax-avoidance structures
