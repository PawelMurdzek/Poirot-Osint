## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Mediterranean migration / pushbacks** — Bellingcat / Lighthouse Reports / Solomon investigations
- **Predator spyware (Cytrox / Intellexa)** — extensive Citizen Lab + Reporters United exposure of Greek-government use against journalists / opposition (2022-onwards)
- **Russian shipping / sanctions evasion** — Greek-flagged shipping in post-Crimea evasion
- **Wagner Group + Greek Cypriot / shipping connections**
- **Predatorgate** — major surveillance scandal
