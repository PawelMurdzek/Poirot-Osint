# Country OSINT — Italy (Italia)

Italy has paid corporate registries (Camera di Commercio is gated) but a **vibrant investigative-journalism scene** and unique resources around organised crime, antimafia, and municipal-level OSINT.

- Regional dialects (Sicilian, Neapolitan, Venetian, Sardinian) — sometimes content-relevant

## Government & Corporate Records

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **Registro Imprese** ([registroimprese.it](https://www.registroimprese.it/)) | Companies registry — paid per-document via local Camera di Commercio |
| **Telemaco / Camera di Commercio** | Corporate filings access — paid |
| **Visura.it / Cerved** | Commercial corporate-data aggregators — paid |
| **Open Corporates Italy** | Free aggregated data |
| **Albo Camerale** | Chamber-of-commerce registers |
| **Anti-mafia legislation lists** | Public — interdittive antimafia (companies blocked from public contracts) |
| **AGCOM** | Communications regulator |
| **CONSOB** ([consob.it](https://www.consob.it/)) | Financial regulator — listed-company filings |
| **Court records** | Limited public access; **DeJure** / **Giurisprudenza Italiana** for legal databases (paid) |
| **Italgiure** | Supreme Court rulings |
| **Open Data Italia** ([dati.gov.it](https://dati.gov.it/)) | National open-data portal |
| **Cadastral / SISTER** | Real estate — paid via Agenzia delle Entrate |

> Italy's **antimafia-related public registries** are unique — companies subject to interdittive antimafia (administrative blocking from public contracts) are publicly listed. Major OSINT angle for organised-crime research.

## Italian-Specific OSINT Patterns

- **Antimafia / organised-crime OSINT** — DIA (Direzione Investigativa Antimafia) reports, parliamentary commission reports, court ruling databases. Massive corpus for OSINT-based crime research.
- **Italian sanctions evasion / Russian-Italian business** — covered by IRPI / Domani regularly
- **Catholic Church OSINT** — Vatican opacity is a sustained journalism field
- **Football / Calcio governance** — corruption / match-fixing investigations recurring
