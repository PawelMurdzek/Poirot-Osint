# Country OSINT — Egypt (مصر)

Most populous Arab country, central to MENA OSINT, severely-restricted press environment under Sisi government, frequent intersection with Israel-Palestine and Sudan / Libya OSINT.

---

## Languages

- **Arabic** — Modern Standard Arabic + Egyptian Arabic (Masri/Ammeya)
- Egyptian Arabic is the **most widely-understood Arabic dialect** globally because of Egyptian film/TV exports
- **English** — widely used in business, urban areas
- **Coptic** — religious / liturgical, minimal modern social presence
- Translation: standard tools; Egyptian Arabic is well-served by Google Translate / DeepL relative to most other dialects

- **Rabaa massacre (2013) verification** — community OSINT
- **GERD / Nile water dispute** with Ethiopia — Sudan, Ethiopia overlap
- **Russian sanctions evasion via Egyptian intermediaries**
- **Suez Canal traffic** — major maritime OSINT point
- **Gas / energy OSINT** (East Med fields)
- **Sisi family business networks** — sustained Mada Masr work
- **Pegasus** — documented use against Egyptian dissidents

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Press freedom severely restricted** — Egypt regularly ranks among worst globally for journalist imprisonment
- **Anti-Terrorism Law (2015)** — broad, used against journalists / activists
- **Cybercrime Law (2018)** — broad
- **Spreading false news** charges used routinely
- **Foreign-analyst risk** — significant; entry-bans, detentions of foreign journalists periodic
- **State surveillance** — Pegasus + Predator + native capability documented
- **Egyptian state actively conducts foreign OSINT** including against diaspora
