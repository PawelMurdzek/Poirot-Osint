# Country OSINT — Malaysia

Multi-ethnic / multi-language Southeast Asian country with English-language access, substantial Muslim majority, hub for SE Asia regional OSINT including 1MDB-scandal corporate work.

---

## Languages

- **Bahasa Malaysia** — official; ~80% mutually intelligible with Bahasa Indonesia
- **English** — co-official in usage, dominant in business
- **Mandarin Chinese, Tamil** — major minority languages
- **Google.com.my** — dominant

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **PDPA Malaysia** — data protection; reform in progress
- **Sedition Act** — broad
- **Anti-Fake News Act 2018** (repealed 2019, partially restored)
- **Press freedom mixed** — improved post-2018 but periodic legal pressure
- **Religious / racial sensitivity** — speech restrictions on these topics
- **Political surveillance** — periodic episodes
