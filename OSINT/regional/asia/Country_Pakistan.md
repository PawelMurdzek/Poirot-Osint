# Country OSINT — Pakistan (پاکستان)

Pakistan has a complex OSINT environment: distinct from [[Country_India|India]] in language, ecosystem, and politics; deeply relevant to Afghanistan, Iran, Saudi Arabia, China-CPEC, and counter-terrorism contexts.

---

## Languages

- **Urdu (اردو)** — national language, RTL Arabic script
- **English** — co-official, dominant in business + government documents
- **Punjabi (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ / پنجابی)** — most-spoken regional language; written in both Shahmukhi (Arabic) and Gurmukhi (depending on region/border)
- **Sindhi, Pashto, Saraiki, Balochi** — regional
- Translation: Google Translate; quality varies for Urdu

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **CPEC (China-Pakistan Economic Corridor)** — sustained Chinese-investment + counter-terrorism + provincial-politics OSINT
- **Counter-terrorism** — TTP / Baloch insurgency — extensive Bellingcat / academic work
- **Cricket-corruption / fixing investigations**
- **Pegasus / spyware** — documented use against Pakistani journalists, opposition (Citizen Lab)
- **2022-2024 PTI / Imran Khan crisis** — heavy social-media monitoring; X/Twitter ban context
- **Floods / climate disasters (2022)** — community OSINT
- **Cross-border with Afghanistan** — drone strike documentation, Taliban contacts

---

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Press freedom** — heavily restricted; Pakistan ranks low globally for journalist safety
- **Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA)** — broad, used against journalists
- **Defamation** — civil + criminal
- **State surveillance** — ISI / FIA active; OPSEC matters for sensitive subjects
- **Pegasus** — documented
- **VPN environment** — sometimes restricted; X/Twitter blocking pushed VPN usage massively up post-2024
