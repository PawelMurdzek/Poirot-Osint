# Country OSINT — Singapore

City-state with outsize OSINT relevance: **regional financial / shipping / commodity hub**, transit point for sanctions-evasion and shell-company structures, English-language, exceptionally efficient public records.

## Why Singapore Matters for Regional OSINT

- **Sanctions evasion / shell-company nexus** — Singapore-incorporated entities are frequently in OCCRP, Bellingcat, RUSI investigations, especially around Iran, DPRK, Russia
- **Commodity trading hub** — Trafigura, Vitol, Glencore Singapore offices
- **Maritime/shipping** — Strait of Malacca + Port of Singapore are AIS-OSINT goldmines; many sanctioned-vessel STS transfers happen in/near SG waters
- **Financial-crime investigations** — many large global probes touch Singapore branches (1MDB, Wirecard)
- **Dark-web / cybercrime SE-Asia hub**

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **POFMA (Protection from Online Falsehoods and Manipulation Act, 2019)** — used to issue correction directions; affects what can be published
- **Defamation** — civil and criminal, claimant-friendly
- **Sedition Act** — broad
- **Internal Security Act** — preventive detention possible
- **Press freedom restricted** — RSF ranks Singapore low for SE Asia
- **Foreign-analyst risk** — relatively manageable, but POFMA notices have hit foreign outlets
- **Singapore intelligence (ISD)** is active
