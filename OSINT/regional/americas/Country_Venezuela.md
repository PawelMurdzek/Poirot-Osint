# Country OSINT — Venezuela

Spanish-language country in deep political / economic / humanitarian crisis. Authoritarian Maduro government, mass emigration (~7.7M+ Venezuelans abroad as of 2024), heavy sanctions context. Most independent press operates from exile.

---

## Languages & Search

- **Spanish (Español)** — Venezuelan variant
- **Indigenous languages** — minor online presence
- **Google.com.ve** — accessible from Venezuela; many sites geo-blocked outside

---

## Social Networks

- **WhatsApp** universal among Venezuelans (in-country + diaspora)
- **Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube** — heavy use; **X / Twitter periodically blocked** depending on political situation
- **Telegram** — growing including opposition organising
- **Facebook** — declining
- **Diaspora-heavy social-media activity** — most political discourse happens via diaspora

## Government & Corporate Records

> Venezuelan public records are **largely opaque and dysfunctional**. Many systems are broken, paywalled, or politicised.

| Source | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **SAREN** | National registries authority — corporate, real estate, civil — paid; access often impractical |
| **SENIAT** | Tax authority — RIF (corporate ID) lookup |
| **SUNAVAL** | Securities regulator |
| **BCV (Banco Central)** | Central bank |
| **CNE (Consejo Nacional Electoral)** | Election Commission — disputed electoral data |
| **Asamblea Nacional** | Parliament — depends on which "AN" (parallel legislatures have existed) |
| **TSJ (Tribunal Supremo de Justicia)** | Supreme Court — government-aligned |
| **Gaceta Oficial** | Government Gazette |

## News & Media

> Venezuelan press freedom is severely restricted. Many independent outlets have been shut down, blocked, or relocated abroad.

### State / state-aligned
- **Telesur** — left-international, Venezuelan + ALBA bloc
- **VTV** — state TV
- **Últimas Noticias, El Universal** — once-independent, now state-aligned

### Independent (in-country, often blocked)
- **El Pitazo** ([elpitazo.net](https://elpitazo.net/)) — investigative, frequently blocked
- **Efecto Cocuyo** ([efectococuyo.com](https://efectococuyo.com/)) — major independent
- **Tal Cual** — opposition-leaning
- **Crónica Uno** — left-critical of Maduro

### Exile / diaspora
- **Armando.info** ([armando.info](https://armando.info/)) — investigative, exile-based
- **Connectas Venezuela**
- **Runrunes**
- **OCCRP Venezuela coverage**

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **Sanctions evasion / Russian-Venezuelan oil** — sustained OCCRP / RUSI / Bellingcat work
- **Iran-Venezuela cooperation** — military, fuel, financial workarounds
- **Mass migration tracking** (Venezuelan diaspora flows to Colombia, Peru, Chile, US, Spain)
- **CITGO / PDVSA US-court litigation** — massive corpus of US court documents
- **Maracaibo / Falcón clandestine fuel trade**
- **Wagner Group / Cuban intelligence in Caracas** — periodic attention
- **2024 election fraud** — community OSINT around tally sheets / actas

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Press freedom severely restricted**
- **Anti-Hate Law (2017)** — broad
- **VPN use is common, partly criminalised in some interpretations**
- **State surveillance** — Cuban-trained, Russian-equipped
- **Foreign-analyst risk** — entry-bans, occasional detentions for journalists; in-country work hazardous
- **Financial OSINT / sanctions** — critical for understanding parallel-economy flows
