| **Banco de la República** | Central bank |
| **Diario Oficial** | Government Gazette |
| **Rama Judicial** ([ramajudicial.gov.co](https://www.ramajudicial.gov.co/)) | Court system |

## Conflict / Crime OSINT

Colombia is OSINT-rich for:
- **FARC dissidents, ELN, Clan del Golfo (AGC)** — armed-group monitoring
- **Cocaine economy + sanctions** — narco-finance OSINT
- **JEP (Special Jurisdiction for Peace)** — transitional-justice public hearings, OSINT-relevant
- **Venezuelan-border smuggling**
- **InSight Crime** — Colombia coverage
- **Colombian armed-forces / "false positives" cases**

## Notable OSINT Patterns

- **2016 Peace Accord + post-conflict OSINT** — JEP public processes
- **Pegasus** — documented use in Colombia against journalists
- **Venezuela-Colombia border crisis** — migration + smuggling
- **Russian / Wagner Latin-America presence** — periodic attention
- **Coca-cultivation satellite OSINT** — UNODC + academic

## OPSEC / Legal Notes

- **Habeas Data Law / Statutory Law on Personal Data Protection** — GDPR-aligned
- **Press freedom under pressure** — journalists frequently threatened, particularly in conflict zones
- **Defamation** — civil + criminal
- **Petro government era (2022-)** has been somewhat journalist-friendly but security-environment risks persist
